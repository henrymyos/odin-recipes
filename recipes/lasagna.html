Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a great dinner for the whole family that always tastes great. It is the perfect meal for a get together with the family. Its soft and warm and has an amazing combination of flavor. It is my one of my personal favorites!

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese Mixture
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles
  2. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish and add 4 boiled lasagna noodles
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top and add 1.5 cups of meat sauce
  4. Repeat the same layers two more times
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese
  6. Cover and bake at 375Â° for 30 minutes. Then remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes
