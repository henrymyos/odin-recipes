Apple Crisp

Description

Apple Crisp is a creamy mixture of apples, oats, and sugary sweetness. It is the perfect dessert for a special occasion or holiday Its soft and warm, but with some cold ice cream creates the perfect taste of hot and cold. It is my one of my family's favorite desserts!

Ingredients

  • sliced Apples
  • Oats
  • White and Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • butter

Steps

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Stir in sour cream and can of soup
  3. Fold in the potatoes
  4. Put mix in baking dish and cover with the cheddar cheese
  5. Cover with crushed potato chips
  6. Put in the oven for around an hour
