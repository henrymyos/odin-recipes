Apple Crisp

Description

Apple Crisp is a creamy mixture of apples, oats, and sugary sweetness. It is the perfect dessert for a special occasion or holiday Its soft and warm, but with some cold ice cream creates the perfect taste of hot and cold. It is my one of my family's favorite desserts!

Ingredients

  • sliced Apples
  • Oats
  • White and Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Combine sliced apples, white sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla extract into a bowl
  3. Pour into a baking dish
  4. Combine flour, oats, brown sugar, salt, cinnamon, and melted butter into a bowl
  5. Sprinkle mixture on top of apples
  6. Put in the oven for 45 to 60 minutes
