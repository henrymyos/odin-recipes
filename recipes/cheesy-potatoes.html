Cheesy Potatoes

Description

Cheesy Potatoes is a creamy mixture of potatoes and cheese. It is the perfect meal for a special occasion or holiday Its hard and crunchy on top while being sweet and savory on the bottom It is my one of my family's favorite meals!

Ingredients

  • Frozen diced potatoes
  • butter
  • Sour Cream
  • Cream of Chicken Soup
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Potato Chips

Steps

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Stir in sour cream and can of soup
  3. Fold in the potatoes
  4. Put mix in baking dish and cover with the cheddar cheese
  5. Cover with crushed potato chips
  6. Put in the oven for around an hour
